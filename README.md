# Empathy Visual Novel

A short interactive visual novel built in Ren'Py that explores empathy, active listening, and how small conversational choices affect others.
[Download](https://github.com/magnusyunus7/EmpathyVisualNovel/releases/download/v1.1/EmpathyProject-v1.1-pc.zip)

---

## Overview

You play as Gary, having a conversation with your friend Fred, who is quietly struggling.

Through a series of dialogue choices, the game challenges you to:
- Notice subtle emotional cues
- Respond without rushing or “fixing”
- Understand the difference between helping and unintentionally shutting someone down

The game is designed as an **interactive experience to promote prosocial behaviour and empathy**.

---

## Learning Objective

This project is based on the idea that empathy is not just about being kind, but about:
- Listening without interrupting
- Avoiding premature judgement or solutions
- Recognising underlying emotional states

Players learn through:
- Making choices
- Observing consequences
- Replaying the same scenario with new understanding

---

## Gameplay

- 6 key decision points
- Each correct choice contributes to a “good ending”
- Multiple endings:
  - Good Ending
  - Mixed Ending
  - Bad Ending

Choices are not always obviously right or wrong — some responses may feel supportive but lead to unintended outcomes.

---

## Survey & Testing

The game includes:
- Pre-game questions (baseline empathy)
- Post-game reflection questions
- Open-ended responses for qualitative feedback

User testing was conducted with at least 5 participants to evaluate:
- Emotional impact
- Clarity of choices
- Perceived realism
